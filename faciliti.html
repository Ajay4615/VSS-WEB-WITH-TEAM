Vidyarthi Sahayyak Samiti, the work of Vidyarthi Sahayyak Samiti is seen as an organization that transforms the lives of thousands of students in rural areas and as a center for youth transformation. The Committee is a body registered under Section 50A (3) of the Public Trustee Act, 1950 (No. E-219, Pune) with the Office of the Charity Commissioner and has jurisdiction over the whole of India. Students who are economically weak in the society but are striving towards something with the strength of merit-Senior Mathematician, Dr. Achutrao Apte, freedom fighters Haribhau Phatak, Sumitratai Kerkar etc started this work in 1955. Samiti is not just lodging and boarding but here the concept of personality development along with affordable accommodation, food arrangement is of utmost importance. The committee works on the three principles of cleanliness, equality and labor dignity.


Room

Three or four students are arranged in a room depending on the size of the room. Each room has facilities such as cot, mattress, table, chair, cupboard, fan etc.


Canteen :- It is mandatory for the student to take food here after admission in the committee. There are two meals in the morning and evening



Library :- Hostel has separate library facility. Sequential and sequential books may be available. Efforts are made for students to do extra reading.



Computers:- There are well equipped computer labs for students. Students can use this lab for course related activities. Some courses on it are also taught with expert advice.
