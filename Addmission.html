Information Sheet ( 2022 ) The aim of the Vidyarthi Sahayak Committee is to provide accommodation and food at a low cost to the poor, intelligent and promising students coming to Pune from rural areas for higher education and to develop their personality and to give them the touch of entrepreneurship. So that the students can get education easily, they can get proper support and guidance and stand on their own feet with self-reliance. This work was started in 1955 under the guidance of Achyutarao Apte. There are five hostels of the institute, two for students and three for female students, and 800 students are benefiting from these facilities. A branch of the committee has also been started in Ahmednagar. The organization is running on financial support from donors and small fees from students. cleanliness, Equality and labor dignity are the trinity of the organization. The organization is making continuous efforts to make responsible citizens for the country. Seats are available for fresh admission every year for male and female students. Admission to each session is by viewing the hostel. After 10th, Diploma, I. T. Admission is given to courses after I., XII. The academic qualification for admission should be at least 60%.

Hostel Rules:


  1. The room has table, chair, cot, cupboard, mattress, curtains and mattress cover. Satranji, bed sheet, plate, file are given to everyone free of charge. After admission, students carry buckets, mugs, clothes hangers, locks etc. Bring the item.
  2. The maximum annual income limit of parents for admission is 2.5 lakh rupees. Admission is considered on the basis of family financial situation and dependents of the family.
  3. Careful use of the facilities provided in the hostel is expected, any damage is compensated by the student concerned.
  4. Prior permission from the supervisor is required if relatives want to visit the dormitory or room. Only if the college has a holiday, one can go to the village with permission once in a session. Cannot go to village every month or every Saturday-Sunday.
  5. Limited use of mobile phones is allowed and no use of mobile phones is allowed after 10 pm, room mates may be disturbed.
  6. The consent form in the admission form must be filled. If any falsehood is found after admission, admission may be cancelled.
  7. It is mandatory for the students to undergo medical examination from the health center of the committee after admission.
  8. All items brought by students are their own responsibility.
  9. Cancellation of admission due to misbehavior or leaving the hostel without clearance is non-refundable.


Activities:


  1. 1. Daily half an hour of yoga, participation in earn learn scheme and attendance of students in some programs is mandatory.
  2. 2. A local donor is given as a parent to visit the student every month to teach public relations and proper manners.
  3. 3. One has to fill the claim/clearance form and leave the hostel on the second day after the exam.
  4. 4. Students are required to work half an hour daily for the hostel. This includes cleaning, fundraising, canteen work etc. Students are observed and guided every month.


    5. Restaurant:


    1. It is mandatory to follow the rules of the restaurant. Outside food and persons cannot be brought into the restaurant.
    2. Students are required to return to the hostel at canteen time at night. In case of delay, permission of supervisor should be taken.
    3. Students working in canteens should work regularly and take leave only by making alternative arrangements. Non-Resident (Dining only) students have to work in canteen for 1 hour daily.


    Admission:


    After reading the rules, students should go to the click here button and fill the entry form correctly and then submit the form by paying the form fee of Rs 100. A message will be sent for interview from the committee. After that the student and their parents will be interviewed. At this time quality is also considered along with financial status. Fee payment is allowed only after admission is confirmed. Original documents should be kept with you at the time of interview. Certificate from Tehsildar / concerned officer of financial income of the family or current pay slip showing all allowances if parent is employed Extract of 7/12 and 8/A if agriculture. The application form should be accompanied by a certificate from the Talathi of the village for the amount of food and accommodation fee concession for students from drought, unseasonal rain, hailstorm-affected villages while taking admission. The final decision to admit the student rests with the committee management.
