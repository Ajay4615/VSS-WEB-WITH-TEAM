• Vidyarthi Sahayyak Samiti •

It is an organization that transforms the lives of thousands of students in rural areas. The work of Student Aid Committee is seen as a center of youth transformation. The Committee is a body registered under Section 50A (3) of the Public Trustee Act, 1950 (No. E-219, Pune) with the Office of the Charity Commissioner and has jurisdiction over the whole of India. Senior Mathematician Dr. Dr. Achutrao Apte, freedom fighters Haribhau Phatak, Sumitratai Kerkar etc started this work in 1955. At that time, the work started by providing meals to five students at different homes. Next, the issue of students' accommodation was sorted out. Then facilities were provided for female students. Now the committee has four hostels in Pune and 747 male and female students are availing the facilities there. Samiti is not only Lodging and Boarding but here the concept of personality development along with affordable accommodation, food arrangement is given utmost importance. The concept of caste, religion, creed is not given support in the committee. The committee works on the three principles of cleanliness, equality and labor dignity. Although the donors are from urban areas, admission is given only to needy and promising students from rural.
